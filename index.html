Undergrad Research Student - Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology (NMIT), Bengaluru

A customized Finite Element Method (FEM) model was developed and implemented in MATLAB to study the dynamic behavior of three-phase smart magneto-electro-elastic (MEE) composite plates, specifically investigating the critical influence of the piezoelectric interphase thickness. Advanced numerical techniques were applied to formulate the coupled eigenvalue problem, allowing for the computation of natural frequencies and mode shapes under various boundary conditions, while Reddy's Third-Order Shear Deformation Theory (RTSDT) was incorporated to ensure accurate structural modeling. The computational framework was rigorously validated, establishing key relationships between material properties and the mechanical response of the composite structure.
